About Me

Hi,
I'm Julian the developer of Flat Shadow, 19 years old.
In a little village in Austria is where the creativity lives. I have always loved being creative and designing stuff from scratch. Since the moment I got my first Computer I have tried to establish texture packs and now I'm creating my masterpiece Flat Shadow. Due to my interest in Illustrations, Flat Shadow consists of the unique style of vector graphics.

Vector graphics are images, which are made of primitive surfaces and graphs and therefore include no pixels. Minecraft, a game known as a pixel game, turns out pixel free with the usage of vector graphics.
